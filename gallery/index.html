"Is your annular ring quietly killing your yield?"

Target 6+ mil annular rings on vias (not the EDA default). That single rule change can improve first-pass yield by 10-15% and eliminate the most common DFM rejection we see.

Quick rule of thumb: take your fab house's minimum annular ring spec and add 2 mil. That's your new design rule.
